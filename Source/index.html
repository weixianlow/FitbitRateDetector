Welcome

Fitbit HeartRate detector utilizes your Fitbit device's heart rate sensor to monitor your heart rate every hour and keep tracks of your heart rate. In the case of our application receives more than 24 hours of zero value heart rate, we would send out notification of your possible death to email address that you have provided during the setup of your account. A copy of your important document uploaded to your account will also be forwarded together to the email address you have provided.

To begin, just login with your Google account on the right side of this page and we will take you through the setups needed to get you all setup!

Please login to the dashboard using a Google account.
